Life Saver Bag

During a natural disaster, the first concern is to save the individuals who have been affected and to supply them with the necessities for survival. Food packets, potable water, essential equipments  and medicine are all included in the "Real Time Life Saver Bag." The system informs the server on the availability of food and the bag's location on a regular basis. Dual alerts are sent to the users when flood is expected. The data from the bag will be used by the rescue teams to conduct an effective analysis of the situation and, as a result, suitable steps will be performed. A pair of gloves is included with the bag.A pulse sensor unit is located on the left glove. If there is no pulse, the server receives an immediate alarm. This information can be used to calculate the fatality rate. This invention is a comprehensive flood-aid system that includes flood alerts, a supply of vital commodities such as food, tools, and medicine, water storage, a water purification unit and a user-server communication system.
